About Dataset

Contents

The feature set includes:
1. Cement: Cement (kg in a m3 mixture)
2. Blast Furnace Slag: Slag Produced in Blast Furnace (kg in a m3 mixture)
3. Fly Ash: Amount of ash produced (kg in a m3 mixture)
4. Water: Amount of water required (kg in a m3 mixture)
5. Super-plasticizer: rigidity of cement after drying (kg in a m3 mixture)
6. Coarse Aggregate: The coarse nature of the cement particles (kg in a m3 mixture)
7. Fine Aggregate: Fineness of the cement (kg in a m3 mixture)
8. Age: Age or time before it needs repairing (in days)

The target set is:
9. Strength of the Cement: Strength of concrete (/kN) per kiloNewton
